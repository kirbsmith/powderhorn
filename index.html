Powderhorn Outfitters

The Finest Fishing and Hunting Experience

Powderhorn Outfitters has been servicing the outdoorsmen of Cape Cod since 1978. Who better to assist the fishermen, hunters, and shooting sports enthusiasts than those who participate themselves! Our knowledgeable team can assist beginners and experts alike. From first time firearm folks to gun enthusiasts, trout fishermen or tournament bound tuna tanglers, or back yard family archers to hard core hunters, we've got you covered! We pride ourselves in honest transparent communication in every aspect of our operation. Owner Mark Cohen founded Powderhorn to share his passion with those around him. It's a regular occurrence for customers to visit the shop with a story of learning to shoot from Mark, buying their first gun here, or getting their first fishing rod at Powderhorn. As industry professionals, our team stays on the forefront of ever-changing laws, hunting and fishing regulations, and upcoming product releases. Our in-house firearms instructors boast more than 50 combined years of teaching experience. It is our goal to continue to foster relationships with prospective and seasoned outdoorsmen throughout the Cape and Islands!
